About Us

The victory Spring Academy was established in 2025 in Surulere. I welcome our dear parents and children to Victory Spring Academy, a family-oriented school wonderfully designed for children between the ages of 18 months and 11 years.

Victory Spring International School has been set up with a mandate to train and develop well rounded pupils and students who will affect and change their local and global community positively. The school will work to ensure every child is given the opportunity to attain his or her God given talent

We very well understand the challenges of selecting a good school for your children hence the school has put in place adequate resources to ensure your expectations are well catered for.

The school believes strongly in the impact of the parents or guardians in grooming a child. Hence, the school will constantly be engaging our wonderful parents/guardians while the children are in our school. We happily welcome our fresh students and parents to our school and hope they find the joy of working with Victory Spring Academy to ensure we create the leaders in our children.

Our School

The pre-school caters to children from ages 3 months to 5 years. Children are encouraged to explore, discover and gain understanding through structured play and learning activities There are five classrooms each with mixed age groups. Each has a directress, two class assistants and a nanny for toddlers.

Our teaching curriculum is based on five Montessori subject areas: language, Mathematics, sensorial, practical life exercise and cultural. Our children follow these Montessori teaching curricula starting with the practical life exercise designed to develop physical control, coordination and concentration skills,

moving through the Montessori sensorial materials which teach shapes, colours dimensions, and relative sizes. They are moved into materials that develop their ability to speak, listen, read write and number work.

Reading is taught phonetically (using jolly phonics) and many of our children are reading simple books and doing addition, and simple subtraction by the time they move into the Elementary school. We also expose them to subjects like music, French and computer. We also help them to explore and investigate through themes and country of focus

Victory Spring Academy is committed to providing a nurturing and stimulating learning environment where every child is supported to reach their full potential academically, emotionally, socially, and morally.
