CURRICULUM

At Victory Spring Academy, our elementary curriculum inspires young learners through hands-on learning and critical thinking, helping them become confident, curious, and capable students.

Our Curriculum Focuses On:

  • Language Arts: Reading, writing, comprehension, and communication skills
  • Mathematics: Problem-solving, reasoning, and number concepts
  • Science: Exploration, experiments, and understanding the natural world
  • Social Studies: History, geography, culture, and community awareness
  • Arts & Creativity: Music, visual arts, drama, and creative expression
  • Physical Education: Physical Education: Fitness, coordination, teamwork, and healthy habits
  • Character & Life Skills: Fitness, coordination, teamwork, and healthy habits

Victory Spring Academy is committed to providing a nurturing and stimulating learning environment where every child is supported to reach their full potential academically, emotionally, socially, and morally.
